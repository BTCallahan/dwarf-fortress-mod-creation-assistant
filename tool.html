Attacks Explanation:
  1. Edge/Blunt: Whether this weapon has a sharp surface that cuts or pierces into an opponent or not
  2. Contact area: This is high for slashing attacks, and lower for stabbing and bludgeoning attacks
  3. Pentration size: This is high for stabbing attacks, and low for slashing attacks. It is ignored for blunt attacks
  4. Verb 2: The transitive verb used to describe the action
  5. Verb 3: The indicative verb used to describe the action
  6. Noun: The noun used to describe the part of the weapon used in the attack. Optional. If blank, 'NO_SUB' will be used instead
  7. Velocity multiplier: How much to multiply the velocity by
  8. Preperation time: How long it takes to prepare to execute an attack
  9. Recovery time: How long it takes to recover from executing an attack
  10. Multiple strike penalty: The penalty (if any) for multiple strike attacks
