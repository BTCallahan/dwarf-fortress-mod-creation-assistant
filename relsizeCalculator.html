Body Part Relsize Calculator

Utility: Relsize Calculator

This is a utility to caclulate the 'actual' sizes of a body part. To use it, enter the size of the creature at the top, and add however many new bodyparts that you need. Next enter the sizes of the body parts and how many of each body part they have. When you are done, click the button at the bottom to caclulate the actual size of each body part. For added convenience, each row has a drop down menu that can be used to select a template that will be applied to the row
